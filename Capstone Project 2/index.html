Safety Mitigation Procedures for Drones

2015

During my fourth year of university, I was an intern at the Romanian Civil Aviation Authority. There I was working within the Navigability Department, more specifically with UAVs (Unmanned Air Vechicles). As a result of this internship, I wrote my thesis on Safety Mitigation Procedures for UAVs. What the safety mitigation procedures do?

  • They layout the guidelines for different procedures;
  • Ensure that drone operators ensure their safety and the safety of those around them;
  • Provide flight scenarios for different types of operations.

Impact of Data Analytics in the Law Field

2020

For my Master's Degree Thesis I wanted to observe the impact Data Analytics can have in a field where the human is a major factor. Thus, I decided to pursue an internship at a Law Firm and add cases of economic criminality in a machine learning model. The outcome was that the model could recognise the impact of the human factor and it was considered in the model's decision making process.
